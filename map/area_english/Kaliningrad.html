Gastronomic festival,
Kaliningrad region

From August 30 to September 3, 2017, Kaliningrad hosted the first international Forum-Festival "Gastronomic Tourism and Street Food", organized by KaliningradStreetFood and the International Enogastronomical Center with the support of the World Tourism Organization, the Federal Agency for Tourism, the Government of the Kaliningrad Region, the Federation of Restaurateurs and Hoteliers of Russia and the People of Association. Representatives of state and municipal structures, public associations, business, educational institutions, expert community and journalists from Moscow, St. Petersburg, Gorno-Altaisk, Kaliningrad, Makhachkala, Novosibirsk, Pskov and a number of other regions of Russia, as well as from Belarus, Great Britain, Greece, Denmark, Ireland, China, Lithuania, Mexico, Poland, Portugal and Thailand.
