Gastronomic festival,
Udmurtia

The project "Gastronomic Map of Russia" was launched on August 6, 2017 in the Udmurt Republic. The expedition was greeted by Burana's grandmothers, famous for the whole world.
The most famous dishes of Udmurt cuisine were presented on the table: open cheesecakes with meat, egg, mushroom or vegetable filling - baking, tortillas from sour dough - tobaccos with zyrk, pelmeni from gray dough with radish, with elk, pike, horsetail, Udmurt pies with an apple, beetroot, mushrooms - Sharkan reds, dishes of the Kama cuisine - baked sterlet and pike, kytybey and kokroki with beets.
